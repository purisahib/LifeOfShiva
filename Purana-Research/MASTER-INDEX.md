## FAMILY TREE

```
SHIVA (Paramashiva — the Absolute)
│
├── First Consort: SATI (daughter of Daksha)
│   └── No children born [Sati self-immolated]
│
└── Second Consort: PARVATI (reborn Sati; daughter of Himavat)
    │
    ├── GANESHA (Ganapati, Vinayaka)
    │   • Created by Parvati from bodily paste
    │   • Elephant-headed remover of obstacles
    │   • Lord of all beginnings
    │
    ├── KARTIKEYA (Skanda, Murugan, Subrahmanya, Kumara)
    │   • Born of Shiva's seed → Agni → Ganga → 6 Krittikas
    │   • Six heads, divine general
    │   • Slew Tarakasura
    │
    ├── ASHOKASUNDARI (daughter)
    │   • Born from Kalpavriksha (wish-tree)
    │   • Married Nahusha
    │
    └── AYYAPPAN (from Shiva + Mohini/Vishnu)
        • Worshipped especially in Kerala
        • Sabarimala temple
```

## PHILOSOPHICAL SCHOOLS

| School | Type | Location | Key Idea |
|--------|------|---------|---------|
| Shaiva Siddhanta | Dualistic | South India/Sri Lanka | Pati-Pashu-Pasha; liberation through Shiva's grace |
| Kashmir Shaivism | Non-dual | Kashmir | Pratyabhijna — recognition of Shiva in oneself |
| Lingayat/Vira Shaiva | Devotional | Karnataka | Work is worship; personal linga; no caste |
| Advaita Vedanta | Non-dual | Pan-India | Shiva = Brahman = the universal soul |
| Pashupata | Dualistic | North India | Ancient Shiva sect from sage Lakulisha |
