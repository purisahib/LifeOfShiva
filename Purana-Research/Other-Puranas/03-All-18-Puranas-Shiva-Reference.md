# All 18 Puranas — Lord Shiva References Guide

## The 18 Mahapuranas — Classification

Puranas are traditionally classified into three categories based on their primary deity:

| Category | Attribute | Puranas |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| **Sattvic** (Vaishnava) | Vishnu | Vishnu, Narada, Bhagavata, Garuda, Padma, Varaha |
| **Rajasic** (Brahma) | Brahma | Brahma, Brahmanda, Brahmavaivarta, Markandeya, Bhavishya, Vamana |
| **Tamasic** (Shaiva) | Shiva/Agni | Shiva, Linga, Skanda, Agni, Matsya, Kurma |

*Note: This Padma Purana classification is debated; all Puranas contain content on all deities.*

## Each Purana — Shiva Content Summary

### 1. BRAHMA PURANA
- **Primary:** Brahma (creator)
- **Shiva content:** References to Shiva's cosmic role, Shiva-Brahma relationship
- **Notable:** Stories about the origin of Shiva from Brahma's forehead (Rudra manifesting from Brahma's anger)
- **Verses:** ~10,000

### 2. PADMA PURANA
- **Primary:** Vishnu/Lakshmi
- **Shiva content:** Stories of Shiva and Vishnu worshipping each other; classification of other Puranas
- **Notable:** Contains the Padma Purana's famous statement about Shiva being the greatest Vaishnava devotee
- **Verses:** ~55,000 (one of the largest)

### 3. VISHNU PURANA
- **Primary:** Vishnu (Vaishnava)
- **Shiva content:** Battle of Banasura (Shiva vs Krishna), general Shiva-Vishnu unity passages
- **Notable:** Shiva fights on Banasura's side; Krishna-Vishnu ultimately prevails (Vaishnava narrative)
- **Verses:** ~23,000

### 4. SHIVA PURANA ⭐ (Primary Shiva text)
- **Primary:** SHIVA
- **Shiva content:** EVERYTHING — 457 chapters entirely about Shiva
- **Seven Samhitas:** Vidyesvara, Rudra, Satarudra, Kotirudra, Uma, Kailas, Vayaviya
- **Key content:** All major Shiva stories, 12 Jyotirlingas, 1000 names, philosophy
- **Verses:** ~24,000

### 5. BHAGAVATA PURANA
- **Primary:** Vishnu/Krishna (Vaishnava)
- **Shiva content:** Churning of ocean/Halahala poison, Mohini story, Daksha's yajna, Banasura battle
- **Notable:** Shiva praised as "the greatest devotee of Vishnu" — a high honor in Vaishnava theology
- **Verses:** ~18,000 (12 Cantos)

### 6. NARADA PURANA
- **Primary:** Vishnu (devotion, Bhakti)
- **Shiva content:** Narada's travels and interactions with Shiva; stories of Shiva's grace
- **Notable:** Narada (eternal storyteller) interacts with Shiva in many Puranas
- **Verses:** ~25,000

### 7. MARKANDEYA PURANA
- **Primary:** Devi (Shakti) — contains the Devi Mahatmyam
- **Shiva content:** Shiva as the supreme being; stories of Rudra
- **Notable:** Contains the **Devi Mahatmyam** (Glory of the Goddess) which is about Durga/Parvati — Shiva's consort
- **Verses:** ~9,000

### 8. AGNI PURANA
- **Primary:** Agni (fire) / comprehensive encyclopedia
- **Shiva content:** Temple construction for Shiva, iconography of Shiva, worship rituals
- **Notable:** One of the most encyclopedic Puranas — covers architecture (Vastu), weapons, cooking, grammar, medicine — all linked to divine worship
- **Verses:** ~15,000

### 9. BHAVISHYA PURANA
- **Primary:** Sun (Surya)
- **Shiva content:** Prophecies about Shiva worship in future ages; Shiva-related festivals
- **Notable:** Contains material about future events (Kali Yuga)
- **Verses:** ~14,500

### 10. BRAHMAVAIVARTA PURANA
- **Primary:** Krishna/Brahman
- **Shiva content:** Shiva's role in cosmic creation; Shiva-Vishnu unity
- **Notable:** Views creation as transformation (Vivarta) of Brahma/Brahman
- **Verses:** ~18,000

### 11. LINGA PURANA ⭐ (Second major Shiva text)
- **Primary:** SHIVA (via the Linga symbol)
- **Shiva content:** Entire text — 163 chapters about Shiva, the Linga, and Shaiva philosophy
- **Key content:** Origin of the Linga, five aspects of Shiva, Tandava dance, Shiva Sahasranama
- **Verses:** ~11,000

### 12. VARAHA PURANA
- **Primary:** Vishnu's Varaha (boar) avatar
- **Shiva content:** Shiva-Vishnu relationship; pilgrimage sites sacred to Shiva
- **Notable:** Geography of sacred India including many Shiva tirthas
- **Verses:** ~24,000

### 13. SKANDA PURANA ⭐ (Largest Purana)
- **Primary:** Skanda/Kartikeya (Shiva's son) — fundamentally Shaiva
- **Shiva content:** Extensive — especially Kashi Khanda (Varanasi/Shiva's city), Maheshwara Khanda
- **Key content:** Kashi Vishwanath stories, pilgrimage guides, Shiva's glory in various regions
- **Verses:** ~81,000+ (LARGEST PURANA)

### 14. VAMANA PURANA
- **Primary:** Vishnu's Vamana (dwarf) avatar
- **Shiva content:** Shiva-Vishnu interactions; Shiva's blessings on devotees
- **Notable:** Contains stories from the Shaiva-Vaishnava interface
- **Verses:** ~10,000

### 15. KURMA PURANA
- **Primary:** Vishnu's Kurma (tortoise) avatar — but classified as Shaiva/Tamasic
- **Shiva content:** Shiva's philosophical teachings; Dakshinamurti stories; worship of Shiva
- **Notable:** Contains the **Ishvara Gita** — dialogues of Shiva teaching spiritual knowledge (counterpart to Bhagavad Gita)
- **Verses:** ~17,000

### 16. MATSYA PURANA
- **Primary:** Vishnu's Matsya (fish) avatar — but classified as Shaiva/Tamasic
- **Shiva content:** Parvati's birth story, burning of Kamadeva, Shiva-related festivals
- **Notable:** Contains temple construction guides, iconography of Shiva
- **Verses:** ~14,000

### 17. GARUDA PURANA
- **Primary:** Vishnu/Garuda
- **Shiva content:** Death, after-death, Shiva's role in liberation
- **Notable:** Read at Hindu funerals — covers what happens after death; Shiva as liberator (especially in Varanasi)
- **Verses:** ~19,000

### 18. BRAHMANDA PURANA
- **Primary:** The cosmic egg (Brahmanda — egg of Brahma)
- **Shiva content:** Cosmology with Shiva at center; contains the Lalita Sahasranama (1000 names of the Goddess — Shiva's consort)
- **Notable:** Contains the Adhyatma Ramayana and the Lalita Sahasranama
- **Verses:** ~12,000

## Summary: Which Puranas to Read for Shiva Content

| Priority | Purana | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| ⭐⭐⭐ | Shiva Purana | Primary source — everything about Shiva |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | Linga Purana | Shiva's symbol, philosophy, 1000 names |
| ⭐⭐ | Skanda Purana | Largest collection; Kashi/Varanasi stories |
| ⭐⭐ | Bhagavata Purana | Churning story, Daksha yajna (different perspective) |
| ⭐ | Markandeya Purana | Devi stories (Parvati's fierce forms) |
| ⭐ | Kurma Purana | Ishvara Gita — Shiva's philosophical teachings |
| ⭐ | Matsya Purana | Parvati's birth, iconography |
